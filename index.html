Candle Care

Rituals & Care FAQs

Maximize your candle life and ensure a clean, aromatic burn with these expert rituals.

Absolutely. Before lighting, always trim your cotton wick to approximately 5mm (1/4 inch). This prevents the flame from growing too tall, eliminates smoke and soot splatters, and extends the overall burn time by up to 20%.

On your candle's first light, let it burn for 2 to 3 hours until the melted pool of wax reaches all edges of the jar. Soy wax has a "memory"—if extinguished before a full pool forms, the candle will continue to burn straight down the center (tunneling), wasting the outer wax.

We strongly recommend using a metal candle snuffer or gently dipping the wick into the liquid wax pool (and straightening it back up). Avoid blowing out the candle, as this produces smoke, releases soot particles into your air, and can cause hot wax to splatter.
